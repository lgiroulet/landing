Finally an app for museums seekers!

Tired of spending hours digging on the internet?

Museum Around might be the app you're looking for

Target

People looking for exhibitions around them sorted by categories, localization, opening hours and accessibility.

Painpoint

Information overload issue : dispersal of information on each museum's website making the research difficult and time consuming.

This is where Museum Around take on its full meaning:

Geolocalisation

Use your geolocalisation to find the hottest exhibitions around you.

Online ticketting

Book your tickets directly on the app and use the generated flashcode to skip the line.

Personalization

Scroll through the catalog and/or enter your art preferences for special recommandations matching your tastes.
